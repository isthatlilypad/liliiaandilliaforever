Our Story

Liliia and Illia met on a dating app — just one swipe was all it took. For Liliia, it was her first and only swipe, and somehow, it turned out to be the right one.

Our first date, however, almost never happened — Illia cancelled it just two hours before, and our story nearly ended before it even began. But fate had other plans. After our "second first date," we have been inseparable ever since.

Now, nearly three years later — filled with laughter, love, and tenderness — we are ready to begin our next chapter. Come celebrate it with us!

FAQ

What's the dress code?
Formal attire encouraged.
What's on the menu?
You can look forward to modern Eastern European cuisine. The menu will include cold and hot appetizers, as well as desserts. At the restaurant, you will also be able to choose your first and main course. An open bar option will be available as well.
What time should I arrive?
1:00 PM if you'd like to attend the civil ceremony, or 4:00 PM if you plan to join us directly for the celebration.
Gifts?
As we dream of a honeymoon in Japan, a monetary gift would be the most appreciated. Flowers are always lovely, but if you'd like, we would truly appreciate a bottle of wine instead — something we can share with you over dinner in the future.
